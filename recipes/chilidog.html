Chilli Dog

Description

A hot dog placed in a bun and topped with chili.

Ingredients

  • 1 hot dog sausage
  • 1 hot dog bun
  • 1/4 cup of canned chili

Steps

  1. Boil the hot dog sausage for 5 minutes
  2. Place the hot dog in the hot dog bun
  3. Pour the 1/4 cup of chili on top of the hot dog
